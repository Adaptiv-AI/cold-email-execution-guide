Tech Setup

üìö Resource Bank:

1. Buy "Burner" Domains
Buy 2-3 secondary domains.
Example: If client is acme.com, buy getacme.com, tryacme.com, acme-partners.com.
Important: Set up a "301 Redirect" so if someone types the burner URL into a browser, it goes to the real website.
2. DNS Records (The "Security Guards")
You must add these TXT records to the domain host (Namecheap/GoDaddy):
SPF (The ID Card): v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com ~all
Tells the internet that Google is allowed to send email for you.

DKIM (The Digital Signature): Generated in Google Admin.
Proven cryptographic proof that the email wasn't tampered with.

DMARC (The Rulebook): v=DMARC1; p=quarantine;
Tells servers what to do if an email fails the ID check (Quarantine it).
3. Warm-up Configuration
Connect to Instantly.ai. Enable "Warm-up".
Settings: Ramp up 2/day. Max 25/day. Reply rate 30%.
WAIT 14 DAYS before sending the first sales email.

Operations Phase 03

Copywriting Strategy

Goal: Start a conversation. Do not pitch. Sell the "next step."

‚ö° The 3-Second Mobile Test

Read the email on your phone. If you have to scroll to read it, it is too long. Cut it in half.

The "Triple Tap" Structure

1. The "Pattern Interrupt" Subject
Avoid "Marketing Services" or "Partnership".
Use: "Question for {{firstName}}", "Quick question", "About {{Company_Name}}".
2. Contextual Bridge
Connect the observation to the problem.
"I was looking at {{Company_Name}} and noticed you're using Shopify..."
3. Soft CTA (Call to Action)
Never ask for 15 minutes or a meeting. Ask for permission to send assets.
"Mind if I send over a 2-min video explaining how we fixed this for [Competitor]?"

üö´ The Anti-Spam Dictionary (Do Not Use)

"Guarantee", "Free", "no-risk", "limited time", "$$$", "100%", "Click here", "Buy now"

Launch & Management

üöÄ Ramp-Up Schedule

Do not blast 50 emails on Day 1. Use this schedule per mailbox:

Week 1 (Warmup Only) 0 Sales Emails / 20 Warmup Emails
Week 2 10 Sales Emails / 20 Warmup Emails
Week 3 20 Sales Emails / 15 Warmup Emails
Week 4 (Full Speed) 30-40 Sales Emails / 10 Warmup Emails

Daily Operations

1. Inbox Rotation
Instantly rotates senders automatically. Monitor "Master Inbox" to ensure one domain isn't getting all the bounces. If one domain has high bounces, pause it immediately.
2. The "Positive Signal" Trick
Check your spam folder in the burner accounts. If you see your own warmup emails in Spam, move them to Inbox and mark as "Important". This trains Google's AI that you are safe.
